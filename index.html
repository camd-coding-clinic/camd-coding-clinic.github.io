Do you need coding help?

The Coding Clinic is here to support you! Drop by to get support and resources around everything related to coding and computation: troubleshooting problems, resolving errors, strategizing solutions, and more. Here are some reasons you might want to stop by:

  • Do you need help understanding the fundamentals of programming, or the mechanics of a specific programming language?
  • Are you confused about how your IDE works?
  • Is something stuck in your code, and you don’t know how to fix it?
  • Do you have a design or technical specification for a project, but you don’t know how to translate it into functional code?
  • Did you build a project, but want feedback on its technical design or functionality?

Expert students are available to help with these kinds of questions and more. We’re happy to point you in the right direction on all things coding!
